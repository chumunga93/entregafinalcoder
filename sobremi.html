A bit about Pablo

Chilean, at age 22 I started taking pictures on film with a dusty zenit camera, passed to me by my uncle. I'm trying to make this my full time occupation, by no means I'm a constant person but when it comes to taking pictures the opposite is true. You will see this portfolio has a bit of everything, each theme reflects some period in my life, the free time as psychologhy student in Santiago was spent roaming the streets, taking mostly mediocre shots but eventually I got better at it, at the time of this writing the COVID-19 pandemic is on its late phase (hope so, please), this being the case I've been dedicating more to landscape and studio portraits latety. My biggest milestone is a an exhibition I made in 2018 on the house of culture of Rancagua, the mid-sized city I come from, I really hope to keep getting better throught practice and constan observation. Through the last year I perfected my skilset with motion graphics and editing courses, I am a beneficiary of the SERCOTEC seed fund.
